Grade Encoding Guidelines

  1. Grades in the NEW Grading System must be encoded as A,B+,B,C+,C,D,F,FD,WP.

  5. NE Grades will not be accepted. Students who have not taken exams must be provided the minimum grade. After the exam has been taken, the teacher should file a grade correction form at the Registrar's Office.
